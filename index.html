Tom Sulik is a software engineer based in Seattle, Washington. Tom has lived abroad and studied foreign languages most of his life, and holds a doctorate in cultural anthropology from Brandeis University.

Tom has developed iOS apps to train blackjack players to beat the casino. His ongoing project is to incorporate sociolinguistics into natural language processing.



In his free time, Tom enjoys playing jazz and blues on the tenor saxophone, and translating Spanish and Italian literature.
